How It Works

Four simple steps from contribution to payout

1

Contribute Your Face

Upload a single face-only selfie. Our AI automatically crops, validates, and converts it into an abstract, non-identifiable artistic element. You'll receive clear terms explaining revenue sharing and usage rights.

2

AI Creates the Artwork

Our system automatically selects hundreds of contributor faces and generates a Monet-inspired composition. Each face is abstracted beyond recognition, creating stunning Impressionist art in museum-grade resolution.

3

Single Collector Purchase

Each artwork is listed as "One of One" and sold to a single buyer globally. Once purchased, the artwork is permanently locked. The buyer receives high-resolution files and an official certificate of ownership.

4

Automatic Revenue Distribution

75% of the sale price is instantly distributed among all contributors whose faces appear in the artwork. Payouts happen automatically via Stripe, PayPal, or bank transfer. No manual accounting, ever.

Ethics & Transparency

Privacy, fairness, and artistic integrity built into every line of code

Privacy First

All faces are transformed into abstract, non-identifiable artistic elements. We never use facial recognition, never sell your data, and never use your image for identification purposes. All processing is GDPR-compliant by design.

Fair Revenue Sharing

75% of every sale goes directly to contributors. Distribution is automatic and proportional. If 1,000 faces are in an artwork sold for $10,000, each contributor receives $7.50. The platform keeps 25% to maintain operations.

Your Control

You can opt out at any time. Your face will not be used in future artworks (existing artworks remain as they are). You can request deletion of your data under GDPR rights at any time.

Artistic Integrity

Every artwork is genuinely unique. Our AI generates each piece individually, and once sold, the exact composition can never be recreated. Buyers receive certificate of authenticity and full ownership rights.
