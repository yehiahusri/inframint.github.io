How It Works

Four simple steps from contribution to payout

1

Contribute Your Face

Upload a single face-only selfie. Our AI automatically crops, validates, and converts it into an abstract, non-identifiable artistic element. You'll receive clear terms explaining revenue sharing and usage rights.

2

AI Creates the Artwork

Our system automatically selects hundreds of contributor faces and generates a Monet-inspired composition. Each face is abstracted beyond recognition, creating stunning Impressionist art in museum-grade resolution.

3

Single Collector Purchase

Each artwork is listed as "One of One" and sold to a single buyer globally. Once purchased, the artwork is permanently locked. The buyer receives high-resolution files and an official certificate of ownership.

4

Automatic Revenue Distribution

A portion of the sale price is distributed among all contributors whose faces appear in the artwork. Payouts happen automatically via Stripe, PayPal, or bank transfer. No manual accounting, ever.
